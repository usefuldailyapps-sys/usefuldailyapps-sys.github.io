The idea

Small apps. Sharp purpose.

No infinite scroll. No dark patterns. No accounts you don't need. Just clean tools that do one thing well — for the parts of your day that deserve a little more clarity.

Single-purpose

Each app solves one real problem instead of trying to do everything badly.

Yours, not ours

Your data stays on your device whenever possible. No selling, no spying, no shouting.

Fast and quiet

Lightweight, native, no bloat. Opens in a heartbeat, gets out of your way.

Made by a human

Built by one person who actually uses these apps. Feedback goes straight to the maker.
